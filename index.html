About Me

Hometown

I was born and brought up in Muzaffarpur, a lively town in north Bihar lovingly known as the Land of Litchis. The world-famous Shahi Litchi it’s in the air, in the people, and in the memories it gives you.

The Garibnath Temple, often called the Deoghar of Bihar, brings the whole town together in faith and celebration.

Muzaffarpur isn’t just my hometown—it’s an emotion. No matter where life takes me, a part of me will always belong to this place

Education


I began my schooling at DAV Public School, Muzaffarpur till class 10th.

Completed my 12th from Dolphin Public School, Muzaffarpur.

I pursued my B.Tech from the National Institute of Technology (NIT), Jamshedpur. My years there helped me think critically, collaborate effectively, and manage time efficiently. Peers there enabled me to think one step above. I made few good friends and memories.

Currently, I'm pursuing my MBA in Business Analytics from the Indian Institute of Management (IIM), Ranchi. Learning about business, networking with people, and preparing myself to make meaningful contributions at the intersection of business and technology.
